Snake Water Gun Game

Welcome to the snake water gun game, to play this game please click on the start button below, then input the number of round you wanna play this game for Exaple 5 or 10, then You have to choose between "snake," "water," or "gun" by clicking on corresponding images.The computer also makes a random choice, and the winner of each round is displayed. At the end of all rounds, the overall result is shown.
